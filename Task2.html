MINDERS INFO


Minders is a student activity established in the faculty of Computers and Artificial
Intelligence -Helwan University.
Six years ago, our founder -Eng. Ahmed Elassy- decided to establish a student activity; not only
to fill the gap between the youth and the labor market, but also to present a new generation of
entrepreneurs to the community.
Now, after closing our fifth season, we can say that we have successfully achieved our mission
to spread the entrepreneurship culture between a large class of young people through our
ongoing workshops, events, and partnerships in many important events in the labor market.
Now, you can find us at Helwan University, Cairo University, Misr University for Science and
Technology, The Egyptian Chinese University, and we are still spreading all around the
Egyptian Universities.
